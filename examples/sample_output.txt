============================================================
  Observations:
  - alpha=0 produces shortest path regardless of risk
  - alpha=1 balances length and risk avoidance
  - alpha=5 strongly prioritises safety over efficiency
  - A* always finds the optimal risk-weighted path
  - RRT provides a feasible alternative, useful when
    the search space is too large for exhaustive search
